Mid-Autumn Festival Lotus Root Stir-Fry

A traditional dish to celebrate the harvest moon

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces lotus root
  • 8 wood ear mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup red bell pepper
  • 1/4 cup chicken stock
  • 2 teaspoons oyster sauce
  • Salt, sugar, white pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 6 slices ginger
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2 scallions
  • 1 tablespoon Shaoxing wine
  • 2 teaspoons cornstarch + water

Instructions

  1. Blanch lotus root, mushrooms, and peppers.
  2. Mix sauces and seasonings in a bowl.
  3. Heat oil, cook ginger, then add garlic and scallions.
  4. Add vegetables and stir-fry quickly.
  5. Add wine and sauce mixture.
  6. Add cornstarch slurry to thicken sauce.
  7. Stir-fry briefly and serve hot.

Mid-Autumn Festival Significance

Lotus root represents family unity and abundance. Enjoy this dish with loved ones under the full moon.
